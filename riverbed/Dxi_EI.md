# Digital Experience Index (DXI)

- Make decisions based on big data analytics.
- Track the status of major areas in IT.
- Set goals based on your priorities.
- Follow up with drill-downs into detailed metrics and insights.
- Compare your digital experience against industry benchmarks.
- It helps you:
    - Identify areas that require immediate attention.
    - Measure progress toward business and IT objectives.
    - Benchmark your organization's digital experience against peers.
    - Drill down from high-level scores into specific applications, devices, or activities.


# Experience Insights
- Prioritize your troubleshooting.
- Direct IT to achieve large scale break-fix goals.
- Address identified productivity impact.
- Focus on severely impacted users and devices.
- It helps in:
    - Identifying issues that impact the most users.
    - Track trends of worsening issues.
    - Target the largest productivity gains.
    - Triage immediately with smart drill downs.
