# Analyze dashboards
- Help us analyze an application deeply.]

## Application dashboards
- Breakdown and filter application data by many different criteria.
- It let's us use diffrent measurements to change views.
- It let's us know: 
    - What are the most heavily used applications, and by whom?
    - What applications have the highest Wait Time and Hang Time as %?
    - How does application performance compare across installed verions?
    - What is the user experience?

## Business Activity dashboard
- It displays performance by application and activity, and find recurring issues.
- Drill down based on many criteria.
- It answers question such as:
    - Which Business activites are the slowest across Business Locations.
    - Are activities faster over VPN, or slower.
    - Which activities are impacting productivity the most?
    - How meaningful are resources like CPU or Memory in activity response time.

## Device Health dashboard
- It lets us Navigate and reort on all collected device attribues (e.g. model, architecture, RAM, location, etc.)
- It detects patterns in specific types of health events in similar types of devices.
- It answers questions, such as:
    - What is the frequence of severe health events across our employee base?
    - What are the most stable Windows OS builds and architectures?
    - Does the Make or Model of our devices impack BSOD or Application crash rates?

## Host Resource dashboard
- Identifies devices with highest CPU or Memory usage.
- Correlate high resource utilization factors such as OS version or Manufacturer.
- It answers questions, such as:
    - Which devices experience sustained levels of high resource usage?
    - Are devices properly sized to support different types of users?
    - Does resource usage differs across various Windows verions?

## Process Resource dashboard
- Identify resource hungry applications.
- Compare resource usages acrosss applications.
- Identify the most efficient application versions
- It answers questions, such as:
    - How much more resources are applications consuming wehn the device hasn't been rebooted?
    - Does the version of an application have any impact on rersouce consumption?

## Remediation Actions dashboard
- Track and measure the progress of Aternity Remediation.
- Report on successsful remediation actions
- Isolate rejected remediation actions.

## Skype for Business users
- Investigate call issues by looking at several key metrics
- Correlate call issues with hardware or software factors
- Skype for Business enables you to quickly answer questions, such as:
    - Which Codecs have the best/worst impact on Call Quality?
    - Do the number of failed or dropped calls change across different Business Locations?
    - How does Call Quality or Video Quality change across different versions of Skype for Business?

## WiFi Dashboard
- Identify issues related to WiFi network and signal.
- Correlate WiFi performance with other User and Device dimensions.
- Compare WiFi performance across Business Locations and WiFi Channels.
- It answers questions, such as:
    - Does WiFi signal strength have any impact on application performance?
    - Which Business Locations have the worst overall signal strength?
    - What access point are users connected to?
