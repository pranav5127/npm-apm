# Taking Action with Detection and Remediation

## Detection (Incidents and Service Desk Alerts)

- Detect **actionable** digital experience issues
- Detect **slow activities** with Incidents
- Detect device and application **health issues** with Service Desk Alerts
- **Follow up:** Review, troubleshoot, notify stakeholders, create IT tickets


## Remediation

- Trigger remote actions in troubled devices
- Trigger automated actions to resolve detected issues
- **Follow-up:** Review actions taken, and their success
