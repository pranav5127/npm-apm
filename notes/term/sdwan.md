# SD-WAN (Software Defined - Wide Area Network)

- SD-WAN technology decouples networking hardware from the control plane.

- It uses software-defined networking to securely route traffic across multiple connection types like MPLS, LTE, and broadband.

- A SD-WAN is more flexible WAN architecture that can take advantage of multiple hardware platforms and connectivity options.

- It can use a range of networking methods for branch connectivity such as <mark>Virtual Private Networks</mark> (VPNs), [MPLS](notes/term/mpls.md), public networks etc.

## SD-WAN architecture components

- SD-WAN Controller: Central system that decides traffic policies and routes.

- SD-WAN Edge: Connects local networks to the SD-WAN and applies controller policies.

- SD-WAN Orchestrator: Manages and coordinates the entire SD-WAN from one dashboard.
